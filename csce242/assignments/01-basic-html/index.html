All Around Guide to Fishing the Keys.

Fishing in Key West is a great experience for beginners because the water is warm, clear, and full of fish. Many people start by fishing from a charter boat, where a captain and crew help with equipment and techniques. You can choose to fish onshore off the beautiful coast, or go deep into the sea. Beginners should use simple gear, such as a basic rod and reel, which is often provided on guided trips.

It is important to listen to the guide and follow safety rules while on the water. Early morning trips are usually best because fish are more active at that time. Wearing sunscreen, a hat, and light clothing helps protect you from the strong sun. Overall, fishing in Key West is a fun and relaxing way to learn the basics while enjoying the ocean.

Fish to Catch!

  • Snook
  • Tarpon
  • Bonefish
  • Permit
  • Redfish
Optimal Weather Best Months to Fish
Warm and calm seas with a light breeze. March to June.
Mild temperatures with steady conditions. September to November.
